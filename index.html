    Welcome to Roosters. If you fancy a Pizza then you have come to the right place as they have some great tasting Pizzas on the menu such as the very popular Rooster Special. If you are a fish lover then order the Mariner topped with tuna, prawns, sweetcorn, tomato, onion and olives or the Sea Food perhaps. Also on the menu are Burgers, Chicken, Snacks and Garlic Bread plus some very good Special Offers and Meal Deals. Order tonight and sample a takeaway from Roosters you will not be disappointed. Roosters opening times are Sunday to Thursday 16:30 – 01:00 and Friday and Saturday 16:30 – 03:00. Orders of £7.99 and over are delivered for FREE, under delivery is not available.
